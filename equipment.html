Our Equipment

Premium sound, heavy-duty build quality, and stunning LED visuals. Discover the hardware that powers the ultimate silent disco experience.

The Headset

Pro-Grade LED Headphones.

We do not use cheap plastic headphones. Our inventory consists of premium, over-ear noise-isolating headsets designed for intense bass response, crystal-clear highs, and supreme comfort.

  • Channel-Sync LEDs

    The glowing rings on the earcups automatically turn Blue, Red, or Green depending on which channel the user is listening to, creating a stunning visual effect across the dance floor.

  • Personal Volume & Channel Control

    Every guest has full control. Simple tactile buttons on the side of the headset allow them to switch between 3 DJs and adjust their own volume instantly.

  • All-Night Comfort

    Engineered with ultra-soft pleather earpads and an adjustable headband, they fit snugly on everyone from kids to adults without causing fatigue.

The Broadcast

Long-Range Transmitters.

The heart of the silent disco. Our transmitters are compact, incredibly powerful, and universally compatible with almost any audio source. No Wi-Fi or Bluetooth pairing is required.

500 Meter Range

Push crystal clear audio across massive festival grounds or through thick building walls.

Universal Inputs

Features standard RCA and 3.5mm Aux inputs to connect easily to laptops, phones, and DJ mixers.

Portable Power

Can be plugged into a standard wall outlet, or run off the internal rechargeable battery for pop-up events.

Mic Integration

Plug a microphone directly into the transmitter for announcements, fitness classes, or hype vocals.

Everything You Need

The Rental Kit.

We don't nickel-and-dime you for cables. Every rental package comes fully equipped in heavy-duty transit bins, ready to plug in and play.

Rugged Bins

Gear arrives in stackable, heavy-duty industrial totes. They protect the equipment during shipping and act as the perfect return-shipping container.

All Audio Cables

We include 3.5mm Aux cables, RCA cables, and Apple Lightning/USB-C adapters so you can connect to literally any device right out of the box.

Multi-Chargers

While headsets arrive 100% charged, we include massive 16-port multi-charging snakes for multi-day rentals or festival weekends.

The Sanitation Standard.

We take hygiene seriously. Our warehouse team follows a strict, multi-step sanitization protocol between every single rental. You never have to worry about receiving dirty gear.

Hand-Wiped Earpads: Every earcup is wiped down with hospital-grade disinfectant.

UV Light Treatment: Headsets pass through a UV-C light sanitation chamber to eliminate 99.9% of bacteria.

Manual QA Inspection: Audio quality, LED functionality, and battery health are tested before packing.

Ready for the Drop?

We guarantee delivery two days before your event. Check our inventory calendars now to secure your equipment before we sell out for the weekend.
