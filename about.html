About Rental Disco

Discover the passion, the tech, and the people behind the nation's premier silent headphone party service.

Our Origin Story

Beat the Curfew.

Rental Disco was born out of frustration in 2019. After spending months planning the perfect backyard party, the police showed up at 10:00 PM due to a noise complaint. The speakers were unplugged, the vibe was killed, and everyone went home early.

We realized there had to be a way to keep the music loud and the energy high without disturbing the neighbors. That's when we discovered the power of silent disco technology. We bought our first batch of 50 headsets, and the next party went until 3:00 AM without a single issue.

Today, we ship thousands of premium LED headsets nationwide, helping event organizers, brides, and backyard hosts throw epic parties that never have to end.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

We are on a mission to redefine how people experience live music and communal gatherings.

Our Mission

To democratize the silent disco experience by making professional-grade audio gear accessible, affordable, and incredibly easy to use for everyday consumers. We strip away the complicated tech and provide a plug-and-play solution so our customers can focus entirely on having fun with their friends and family.

Our Vision

To become the nation's most trusted and recognizable provider of wireless event audio. We envision a future where noise ordinances and curfews are no longer an obstacle to celebration, and where interactive, multi-channel listening becomes the standard for festivals, corporate retreats, and private parties alike.
